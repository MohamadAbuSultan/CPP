what's difference  between 
Data - Information - Knowledge - Wisdom

1) Data VS Information
Example:-
Temperature in the past 100 years. (Data) الداتا دائماً هي عبارة عن حقائق موجودة ومسجلة
Global Temperature is rising. (Information) مشتقة من الداتا ، هي عبارة عن داتا + معنى

Data 															vs 										Information
Raw facts																							Driven from data
Unstructured/Unorganized														Structured/Organized
Unanalyzed																							Analyzed
Unprocessed																					Processed
Has no meaning																				Has meaning

2) Knowledge VS Wisdom
data =(input)> processing =(output)> information =(with time)> knowledge =(Applied)> Wisdom
